The LifeLink Protocol

Terms, Conditions & Safety

LifeLink is a bridge between those who need and those who give. To ensure a safe experience for our community in Dhaka, please follow these guidelines.

01 For Our Donors

Health Verification

Donors must provide proof of health condition when requested. You should be at least 18 years old and weigh over 50kg.

Availability Status

Please keep your status updated. If a requester calls, be honest about your current ability to travel to the hospital.

02 For Requesters

Detailed Information

When posting, you must include the patient's name, the hospital address in Dhaka, and a direct contact number.

The Donation Process

Once a donor contacts you, facilitate their arrival at the hospital. LifeLink is purely for connection; all logistics happen between you and the donor.

Platform Security

Privacy

Your contact number is only visible to registered members to prevent spam and ensure genuine help.

Zero Commerciality

LifeLink is a non-profit initiative. Selling blood or charging for connections is strictly prohibited and will result in a permanent ban.

Reporting

If a user provides false information or behaves inappropriately, report their profile immediately to our security team.
